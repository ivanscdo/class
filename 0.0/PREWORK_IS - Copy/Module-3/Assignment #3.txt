Of the 12 Keys to Success, I believe my greatest challenge will be finding a squad and collaborating, at least initially. I have no problem persevering through difficult situations. I’m very adaptable and disciplined, at least according to my girlfriend. Although I can be introverted, I am not anti-social, or a loner, or shy. Overall, I am comfortable in group situations. The issue is that I can already see myself having trouble initiating that connection or inviting myself to a study group. It’s that first step in making friends, in breaking the ice, that can sometimes be a hurdle for me. The good news is it’s something I’ve always been aware of, but not something that has ever held me back when meeting new people.
